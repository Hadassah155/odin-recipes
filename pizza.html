PIZZA


Pizza is a flat, open-faced baked pie of Italian origin, consisting of a thin layer of bread dough topped with spiced tomato sauce and cheese, often garnished with anchovies, sausage slices, mushrooms, etc.

Ingredients for Pizza

  • Fresh pizza dough
  • Marinara
  • Canadian bacon slides
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Red and green bell pepper, red onion, and sliced black olives round out the toppings.
  • Low-moisture, part-skin mozzarella cheese
  • Great Paremesan chesse and fresh basil

Steps To Prepare a Pizza

  1. Preheat oven to 500Â°F (with a baking stone or baking sheet on the center rack).
  2. Roll or stretch pizza dough to a 14-inch circle, and transfer to a piece of parchment paper.
  3. Top generously with toppings.
  4. Transfer parchment to pizza stone or baking sheet, and bake until bubbly, 9 to 10 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes and sprinkle with Parmesan and basil before serving.
